fixed point iteration algorithm:
1.   Start
2.   Enter the changed function, g(x)
3.   Enter the initial guess, a
4.   Enter the tolerance value, e
5.   Calculate 
	b=g(a)
6.   Find /a-b/
7.   If /a-b/>e
	goto step 5
		else
		goto step 3
8.   Display b as root of the function.
9.   Stop
